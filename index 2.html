🏠 SBSHC Co-op Housing

The cheapest legitimate housing near UCSB. 5 houses, ~100 residents. Average double ≈ $1,000/mo all-inclusive (rent, meals, utilities, internet, supplies). Merton's low-income scholarship drops this to ~$500–700. Must be enrolled at UCSB, SBCC, or any SB County post-secondary school.

📝 How to Apply

Applications accepted on a rolling first-come, first-served basis. Apply ASAP for best chances. Summer 2026: June 19 – Sept 16. School Year 2026–27: Sept 19 – June 15.

MyUniStop — UCSB Off-Campus

myunistop.com

Student-to-student housing board specifically for UCSB off-campus housing. Features include:

• Budget filters — set your price range (e.g. $800–$1,500)
• Room type filters — single, double, triple
• Roommate matching — post your profile and preferences
• Direct messaging between students
• Both seekers and listers — you can post that you're looking, or browse who has spots

Great for finding IV doubles at budget prices since it's student-to-student (no landlord markup).

Open MyUniStop →
🏘️

IV CSD — Renter Resources

Isla Vista Community Services District

Official renter resource hub for Isla Vista. 98%+ of IV housing is renter-occupied, so this is tailored to renters. Key resources:

• Isla Vista Tenants Union (IVTU) — tenant rights education, advocacy, dispute resolution
• Free Rental Housing Mediation & Legal Advice — resolve disputes with landlords
• Model Roommate Agreement — template for splitting costs/responsibilities
• Move-in / Move-out Support — guides for transitions
• Housing forum links — Facebook groups, Craigslist, other listing sites
• Leasing company directory — compiled by AS Pardall Center

🔗 IV CSD Renter Resources → ⚖️ Free Mediation & Legal Advice → 📄 Model Roommate Agreement → 📦 Move-in Support → 📦 Move-out Support →
🎓

UCSB Basic Needs — Affordable Housing

basicneeds.ucsb.edu

UCSB's centralized resource for students facing housing insecurity. Covers on-campus, off-campus, cooperative, subsidized, and emergency housing. Key sub-pages:

• Affordable Housing — links to co-ops, SB Housing Authority workforce program, Duncan Group, city affordable housing programs
• Rapid Rehousing — emergency/transitional housing, Safe Parking Program, shelters, DV housing
• Financial Assistance — Financial Crisis Response Team (FCRT) for emergency grants, GSA Emergency Relief Grants, short-term loans
• Mediation — free landlord/tenant and roommate dispute resolution
• Off-campus rental listings via UCSB Housing Office (requires NetID)
